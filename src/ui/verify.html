How verification works

  1. 0G Storage check — we attempt to download the blob from the 0G storage nodes. Bytes-back = the CID exists permanently on chain, independent of our server.
  2. Local index match — our server records summaries + decryption keys; this shows what we recorded about this CID.
  3. 0G Chain anchor (verdicts only) — we call the WitnessAnchor contract's getVerdict(disputeId); the on-chain root hash must byte-match the CID you pasted.
  4. Decrypt (verdicts & chats in our index) — we decrypt the downloaded blob using the local key and show the original content.

If a third party stood up their own Witness server, only steps 1 and 3 would apply (no local key) — but those two alone prove "the verdict that was published is byte-identical to the blob anyone can fetch from 0G."
